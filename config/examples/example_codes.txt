# This file demonstrates how to define codes in plain text

## Main Theme: AI Adoption Challenges

### Technical Challenges
Description: Issues related to the technical implementation and integration of AI systems
Examples: 
- "The API integration was much more complex than we anticipated"
- "Our legacy systems couldn't handle the data volume"

#### Data Quality Issues (child of Technical Challenges)
Description: Problems with data availability, quality, or preparation for AI systems
Examples:
- "We spent months just cleaning the data"
- "The historical data was inconsistent and incomplete"

#### Integration Complexity (child of Technical Challenges)
Description: Difficulties integrating AI with existing systems and workflows
Examples:
- "Getting the AI to work with our ERP was a nightmare"
- "We had to rebuild several systems from scratch"

### Organizational Resistance
Description: Human and organizational barriers to AI adoption
Examples:
- "People were afraid the AI would replace their jobs"
- "Management didn't understand what AI could actually do"

#### Employee Concerns (child of Organizational Resistance)
Description: Fears and anxieties from staff about AI implementation
Examples:
- "Staff worried about job security"
- "People didn't trust the AI's decisions"

#### Leadership Gaps (child of Organizational Resistance)
Description: Lack of leadership understanding or support for AI initiatives
Examples:
- "Executives had unrealistic expectations"
- "No clear vision from the top"

## Main Theme: AI Benefits Realized

### Efficiency Gains
Description: Improvements in operational efficiency from AI implementation
Examples:
- "Processing time reduced by 80%"
- "Automated tasks that took days now take minutes"

### Decision Enhancement
Description: Better decision-making enabled by AI insights
Examples:
- "The predictive analytics helped us avoid major issues"
- "Data-driven decisions replaced gut feelings"

### Innovation Opportunities
Description: New capabilities and services enabled by AI
Examples:
- "We can now offer personalized services at scale"
- "Created entirely new revenue streams"

## Main Theme: Implementation Strategies

### Pilot Approach
Description: Starting with small-scale tests before full deployment
Examples:
- "We tested with one department first"
- "The pilot helped us identify issues early"

### Change Management
Description: Strategies for managing organizational change during AI adoption
Examples:
- "Extensive training programs were essential"
- "Communication was key to getting buy-in"

### Partnership Models
Description: Working with external partners for AI implementation
Examples:
- "We partnered with a specialized AI vendor"
- "University collaboration brought needed expertise"

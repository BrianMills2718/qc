# Example Speaker Property Definitions
# This file defines what properties to track for speakers in interviews
# The system will extract these properties for each identified speaker

## Required Properties

Role/Position
- Type: Text field
- Description: The speaker's job title or role in their organization
- Examples: "CTO", "Data Scientist", "Project Manager", "CEO"
- Required: Yes

Organization
- Type: Text field  
- Description: The company or institution the speaker represents
- Examples: "TechCorp Inc.", "University of Example", "StartupAI"
- Required: Yes

## Optional Properties

Seniority Level
- Type: Categorical (choose one)
- Options: Junior, Mid-level, Senior, Executive
- Description: The speaker's level of seniority in their organization
- Required: No

Department
- Type: Categorical
- Options: IT, Operations, Marketing, Sales, R&D, HR, Finance, Executive
- Description: The functional area or department the speaker works in
- Required: No

Years of Experience
- Type: Number
- Description: How many years of professional experience the speaker has
- Required: No

AI Experience Level
- Type: Categorical
- Options: Novice, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert
- Description: The speaker's level of experience with AI technologies
- Required: No

Industry Sector
- Type: Text field
- Description: The industry sector of the speaker's organization
- Examples: "Healthcare", "Financial Services", "Manufacturing", "Retail"
- Required: No

Geographic Region
- Type: Text field
- Description: Where the speaker/organization is located
- Examples: "North America", "Europe", "Asia-Pacific"
- Required: No

Areas of Expertise
- Type: List (multiple values)
- Description: The speaker's areas of technical or domain expertise
- Examples: ["Machine Learning", "Data Engineering", "Business Strategy"]
- Required: No

Implementation Role
- Type: Categorical
- Options: Decision Maker, Implementer, End User, Consultant, Vendor
- Description: The speaker's role in AI implementation
- Required: No
